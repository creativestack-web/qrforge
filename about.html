Our Story

QRForge started in 2023 when our founders — a small team of developers and designers — were tired of paying for basic QR code generation tools that were freely available in theory but hidden behind paywalls, watermarks, and mandatory sign-ups.

We built QRForge to be the tool we always wanted: clean, fast, powerful, and completely free. No tricks. No hidden fees. No watermarks on your downloads. Just a genuinely useful tool for everyone.

Today, QRForge serves over 50,000 users every month — from small business owners and restaurant managers to marketing agencies, teachers, and event planners around the world.

Our Mission

To democratize QR code technology by providing a world-class generator that is permanently free, privacy-respecting, and accessible to every person and business on the planet — regardless of budget.

Our Values

🔒

How Do We Stay Free?

QRForge is supported by display advertising (Google AdSense). When you visit our site, you may see relevant ads displayed in designated ad slots. These ads cover our server costs and allow us to keep every feature completely free for all users.

We are committed to keeping ads non-intrusive and relevant. We will never show pop-ups, auto-play video ads, or interstitial ads that interrupt your experience.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is QRForge really free forever? ▼
Yes — 100% free, forever. No hidden plans, no premium tiers, no credit card needed. Generate unlimited QR codes at no cost.
Do generated QR codes expire? ▼
Never. Static QR codes generated with QRForge are permanent. They are encoded directly into the image and will work forever as long as the content they point to remains valid.
Can I use QR codes for commercial purposes? ▼
Absolutely. All QR codes you create are yours — use them on product packaging, business cards, restaurant menus, advertisements, print materials, and more.
What is the difference between PNG and SVG? ▼
PNG is a raster image — great for digital screens, websites, and social media. SVG is a vector format — it scales infinitely without pixelation, making it ideal for print, signage, and large format materials.
What is Error Correction Level? ▼
Error Correction allows QR codes to be scanned even if partially damaged or obscured. Low (L) = 7% recovery, Medium (M) = 15% recovery, High (H) = 30% recovery. Use High if you plan to add a logo over the QR code.
How do I create a WiFi QR code? ▼
Select the "WiFi" tab, enter your network name (SSID), password, and security type (WPA is most common). Generate and download. Anyone who scans it will be connected to your WiFi automatically on most modern smartphones.
Is my data stored on your servers? ▼
No. QRForge generates QR codes entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device and is never sent to or stored on any server. Complete privacy guaranteed.
What size should I choose for print? ▼
For digital use (web, social media), 256px is sufficient. For print materials (flyers, posters, packaging), choose 512px or 1024px. For large format print (banners, billboards), use SVG format as it scales infinitely.
